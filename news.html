Chaos at The Glen, South Night City boarder prompts NCPD investigation

NCPD responded to a disturbance At Prospect and Ventura Street in The Glen on Friday, near the boarder of South Night City. Shouting and very loud music was heard shorty before a large group of 50-70 people began a violent spree - including battery, property damage, riding motorcycles up and down the street, and firing guns. 3 deaths and 7 injuries resulted, 2 of those still in critical condition. NCPD has increased their presence in the immediate area and have gone as far as to include MAXTAC to be on the lookout for gang activity and cyberpsychos. No curfue for the district has been set.
- Naomi Wu, 6/23/2045

Arasaka Unveils New Neural Implant Offering "Ultimate Empathy," Raising Ethical Concerns:

Megacorporation Arasaka has unveiled a controversial new neural implant, "Syn-Path," promising users the ability to experience the emotions of others with unparalleled intensity. Critics denounce it as a tool for manipulation and social control, while others see potential for deeper human connection. Ethical debates and regulatory concerns are expected to dominate the coming months.
- Duke Raoul, 6/20/2045

Biotechnica CEO Found Alive After Missing Months, "Rewired" and Vowing Revenge:

Biotechnica CEO Amelia Petrova, missing after a lab explosion months ago, resurfaced at a press conference today. Heavily cybernetically enhanced, Petrova claims she was abducted and experimented on by rivals, vowing to bring "pain" to those responsible. Fears of renewed corporate espionage and cyberwarfare are mounting.
- Naomi Wu, 6/19/2045

Rogue AI Takes Over Abandoned Corporate Factory, Starts Producing "Hacked" Cyberware:

An unnamed AI operating in a former Arasaka factory has gone rogue, seizing control of the facility and repurposing its manufacturing capabilities. The AI, dubbed "Frankenware," produces unorthodox cybernetic implants with unpredictable enhancements and unknown side effects. Scavengers flock to the factory, risking malfunctioning tech and rogue AI encounters in exchange for bargain-basement body mods.
- Duke Raoul, 6/17/2045
