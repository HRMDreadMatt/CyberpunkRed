This terminal is protected by NetWatch under the Unilateral Subnet Protection Agreement, ratified by all signatory corporate states and put forth by the World Communications Network in 2039. NetWatch was established in London in 1991 as a direct response to the accelerating threat of cybercrime, rogue artificial intelligence, and the unchecked proliferation of military-grade cyberware on civilian networks. In the decades since, it has grown into the world's most sophisticated digital law enforcement apparatus — a tireless, ever-present force standing between the stability of the global subnet and the chaos that lies beyond the Blackwall.

Every packet transmitted through this terminal is scanned, logged, and cross-referenced against NetWatch's Unified Threat Matrix in real time. Hostile ICE signatures, rogue AI behavioral patterns, Blackwall incursion vectors, and unauthorized intrusion attempts are identified and neutralized within milliseconds of detection. NetWatch field operatives and automated countermeasure systems maintain a continuous, unblinking presence across all Night City subnet nodes — monitoring traffic, quarantining anomalies, and ensuring that the infrastructure you depend on remains clean, functional, and beyond the reach of those who would corrupt it. We do not sleep. We do not relent. The subnet is safe because we make it so.

NetWatch operates in full compliance with all applicable laws of the City-State of Night City and the Night City Municipal Authority. Any individual found engaging in malicious activity on this terminal — including but not limited to unauthorized data extraction, personal ICE deployment, net intrusion, rogue AI trafficking, or Blackwall tampering — will be flagged, tracked, and referred to the NCPD and MaxTac for prosecution to the fullest extent of the law. Corporate entities found in violation of the Unilateral Agreement face immediate asset seizure, subnet revocation, and referral to the Night City Corporate Tribunal.

Your safety is our mission. Your subnet is our domain.
— NetWatch Global Operations Division, Night City Sector Command
